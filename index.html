三 字 经

Below is the Three Character Classic, or "San Zi Jing".

Foreign students of China and/or Chinese would do well to study it. For one thing, at least until relatively recently most Chinese people had to memorize it as children. They still have to in some places like Taiwan.

Wang Yinglin (王應麟, 1223–1296) probably wrote it.

Herbert A. Giles did the English translation below in 1910.
